SRS Broadcast Service

Supply a link or upload an audio file and have it broadcast to the specified DCS SRS service.

Supports sources YouTube, SoundCloud, Bandcamp, Vimeo, Twitch streams, Local files, HTTP URLs, and formats MP3, FLAC, WAV, Matroska/WebM (AAC, Opus or Vorbis codecs), MP4/M4A (AAC codec), OGG streams (Opus, Vorbis and FLAC codecs), AAC streams, Stream playlists (M3U and PLS)
